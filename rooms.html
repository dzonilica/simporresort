Frequently Asked

Before You Book

What do I need to bring?+
A current vaccination record and any items that help your dog feel at home. We'll walk you through the rest at booking.
Can I bring my dog's own food?+
It's not required. We have quality food on site, so you don't have to bring any. If you'd prefer to keep your dog on the same brand, you're welcome to bring it.
Which dogs do you accept?+
We work with companion, sport, and working dogs of all breeds. Specific cases are discussed individually before booking so we can plan the right care.
How long can my dog stay?+
From short stays to longer programs — we tailor the plan to your needs. Get in touch and we'll arrange the right length together.
How do I book or make changes?+
Reach out by phone or email and we'll handle the rest. Any changes to your reservation can be arranged directly with us.
