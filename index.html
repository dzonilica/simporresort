Our Story

A Lifetime
With Working Dogs

Simpor began in 1994 with one conviction — dogs do their best work when they live in conditions that respect their biology, their drives, and their individuality. From a single working kennel, we have grown into a complete center for the breeding, training, and care of sport and working dogs.

Today, the Simpor Dog Resort exists alongside our championship kennel. The same hands that prepare dogs for IPO trials care for your dog while you're away.

Our Facility

Designed for Excellence

Simpor Dog Center is a specially designed complex dedicated to the breeding, accommodation, and professional training of dogs. Spread across more than two hectares of organized space, in the natural surroundings of Šumadija, the center provides conditions for safe, functional, and efficient work with dogs according to modern international standards.

Both outdoor and indoor training areas support work in all weather conditions — from obedience and sport disciplines to protection and K9 training.

In the heart of Šumadija surrounded by untouched nature, a space was created that combines experience gained around the world, a natural environment, and functional infrastructure — with one goal: to provide every dog with the best possible conditions for development, work, and progress.

What is Simpor Dog Resort

More Than
Boarding — It's Their Vacation

Simpor Dog Resort is an exclusive part of Simpor Dog Center, created for owners who want their dogs to enjoy the highest level of care, comfort, and safety while they are away. Whether you are traveling, on vacation, or simply need daily care for your dog, Simpor Dog Resort offers a unique environment where every dog receives professional attention, individual care, and the feeling of being at home.

Located in the heart of nature, within more than two hectares of organized and secure space, Simpor Dog Resort combines premium accommodation, professional supervision, and daily activities designed to keep every dog happy, relaxed, and active.

Our goal is simple — while you enjoy your vacation, your dog will enjoy theirs.
