of Cynology

From a single passionate kennel to a complete dog resort — the story of building a place where every working line and family companion gets the same uncompromising standard of care.

Our Story

A Lifetime With Working Dogs

Simpor began in 1994 with a single conviction: dogs do their best work when they live in conditions that respect their biology, their drives, and their individuality. From a working kennel in central Serbia, we have grown into a full center for the breeding, training, and care of sport and working dogs.

Our handlers have travelled, competed, and trained internationally for over three decades. That experience is what defines our standards now — every protocol, every suite, every walk is built on lessons learned from world-class trials, top-tier breeders, and veterinary specialists across Europe.

Today, the Simpor Dog Resort exists alongside our championship kennel. The same hands that prepare dogs for IPO trials care for your dog while you are away. That is the difference.

Philosophy

No Shortcuts, Ever

A dog cannot tell you it is uncomfortable, anxious, or in pain. Our entire approach starts from that responsibility. Every dog that stays with us is read — individually — by people who have spent decades learning to read dogs.

We refuse to scale beyond what we can do well. We will tell you when we are full. We will tell you if your dog is not a fit for group activity. We will phone you if something seems off. There is no auto-renew on care.

The goal is simple: your dog leaves Simpor in at least the same condition as it arrived, ideally a little sharper, fitter, and more confident.

What We Stand For

Four Non-Negotiables

01
Individual Care

No two dogs are the same. Every guest gets a tailored daily plan: feeding, walks, enrichment, and rest — built from a structured intake interview before arrival.

02
Real Cynology

Our handlers are working cynologists, not part-time pet sitters. Every staff decision is informed by decades of trial, breeding, and rehabilitation experience.
